Privacy Policy for GitStats

Effective Date: October 6, 2025

GitStats respects your privacy. This app is designed to let you view your GitHub contribution graph as a widget. We do not collect or share any personal data.

Information Collection

GitStats does not collect or store personal information. Your GitHub username is only used locally to fetch publicly available contribution data from GitHub.

Data Usage

All data is fetched directly from GitHub's public API and displayed locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers.
